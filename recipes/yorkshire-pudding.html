12 Yorkshire Puddings

What you will need

Metric

  • 140g plain flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 200ml of milk
  • cooking oil of choice (sunflower in recpie)

Directions

  • Heat oven to 230c/ fan 210c/ gas mark 8
  • Drizzle a little sunflower oil evenly into two, 4-hole yorkshire pudding tins OR two 12-hole non-stick muffin-tray and place into the oven and heat through
  • To make the batter, tip 140g plain flour into a bowl and beat in 4 eggs until smooth
  • Gradually add 200ml milk and carry on beating until the mix is compleately lump-free. Season with salt & pepper.
  • Pour the batter into a jug, then remove the hot tins from the oven. Carefully and evenly pour the batter into the holes.
  • Place the tins back into the oven and leave undisturbed for 20-25 mins, until the puddings have puffed up and browned.
  • Serve immediately. You can now cool them and freeze for up to 1 month
